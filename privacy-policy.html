Privacy Policy

App Name: TikTok Trends
Last Updated: March 1, 2026

1. Introduction

TikTok Trends ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our automated content creation and posting service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including your TikTok account authorization credentials and content preferences. We also collect operational data such as posting history, scheduled content, and video performance metrics returned by the TikTok API.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to operate the TikTok Trends service. This includes posting content to TikTok on your behalf, retrieving performance metrics for posted content, and improving the quality of generated content over time.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to any third party. We share data only as strictly necessary to operate the service, including with TikTok's API services to enable content posting and analytics, with ElevenLabs solely to generate voiceover audio for your content, and with Pexels solely to retrieve licensed stock video footage used in content assembly.

5. Data Retention

We retain operational data including post history and performance metrics for as long as the service is in use. You may request deletion of all stored data at any time by contacting us at the email address below. Upon request, we will delete your data within 30 days.

6. TikTok API Data

TikTok Trends accesses TikTok's API to post content and retrieve video statistics on your behalf. We do not store or share any TikTok user data beyond what is necessary for the operation of this service. Our use of TikTok API data is governed by TikTok's API Terms of Service.

7. Third Party Services

Our service integrates with the following third party platforms, each of which has its own privacy policy governing their use of data: TikTok (tiktok.com), ElevenLabs (elevenlabs.io), and Pexels (pexels.com). We encourage you to review their policies independently.

8. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information, including storing API credentials in environment variables and never logging sensitive keys. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
